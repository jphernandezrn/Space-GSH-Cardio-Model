# Space GSH-Cardio Model

A comprehensive Python-based modeling suite that explores how multivitamins and magnesium influence intracellular glutathione (GSH) levels, oxidative stress modulation, and cardiac risk reduction during spaceflight conditions.

## Overview

This repository contains the computational models developed for the study:

> **"Modeling Multivitamin-Induced Glutathione Elevation and Magnesium for Cardiac Risk Reduction in Suborbital and Long-Duration Spaceflight: A Simulation Study"**

by Joannes Paulus Tolentino Hernandez, DComm, RN, MA Nursing, CHSE, MSAP(c).

The models integrate conceptual frameworks, pharmacokinetic simulations, and probabilistic risk analysis tailored to microgravity environments.

## Modeling

- **Physiological Modeling**: GSH elevation, Nrf2/PPAR pathway activation, and magnesium's role in cardiac electrophysiology.
- **Pharmacokinetics**: ODE-based absorption models with microgravity-adjusted parameters (enhanced dissolution and absorption rates).
- **Risk Analysis**: Probabilistic Monte Carlo simulations linking radiation exposure + microgravity duration to cardiac risk.
